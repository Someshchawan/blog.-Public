# About me

I am Somesh Chawan, a Senior Information Developer at HCL Software with 7+ years of experience in technical writing, API documentation, and developer experience across SaaS, cloud, and AI platforms.

I write here about the intersection of documentation, AI tooling, and developer experience, specifically about what happens when you try to make AI-generated content trustworthy enough to show to a real user.

## What I have built

**AI Support Copilot** ([source code](https://github.com/Someshchawan/ai-support-copilot) | [documentation site](https://someshchawan.github.io/ai-support-copilot-docs/))

A production-style AI support assistant with an evaluation layer that checks every AI response across 8 quality dimensions before it reaches an end user. The evaluation system catches hallucinations, error leaks, irrelevant responses, and low-quality outputs, backed by 17 automated tests proving each check works.

**Custom Gemini Gem** (built at HCL Software)

An AI tool that automates content structuring, error detection, and refinement in documentation workflows. Cut content planning time by 75% and contributed to reducing support tickets to zero on owned product features.

- The gap between "AI can generate text" and "AI can generate trustworthy documentation"
- Developer experience and onboarding design
